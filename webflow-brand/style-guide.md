### Color Usage Rules
- Each asset should include black, white, and ONE color
- Avoid multiple colors in single illustrations
- No color-based text
- No contiguous colored lines
- No gradients
- No color flooding

## Typography

### Primary Font: WF Visual Sans
Geometric typeface inspired by ITC Avant Garde and Futura. Variable font with lower stroke contrast.

**Headings** — WF Visual Sans Semibold:
- H0: 8rem, line-height 1.04, letter-spacing 0.01em
- H1: 5.313rem, line-height 1.04
- H2: 3.5rem, line-height 1.04
- H3: 2.313rem, line-height 1.04
- H4: 1.5rem, line-height 1.3, letter-spacing 0.02em
- H5: 1rem, line-height 1.3
- H6: 0.9375rem, Medium weight

Multi-line headlines may use Regular weight with Semibold for emphasis.

**Body** — WF Visual Sans Text Regular:
- 1rem, line-height 1.6
- Optical sizing optimized for readability

### Google Font Fallback
- Headings: Poppins Semibold
- Body: Inter Regular

## Illustration Guidelines

### Standard Illustrations
- Simple, dimensional objects suited for written narratives
- Monochromatic palette: one color + black + white
- Use one or two tones for emphasis and hierarchy
- People should have a serious, technical demeanor (like medical illustrations — not cartoonish)

### Elevated Illustrations
- Bridge between standard and aspirational styles
- Feature characteristic "bloom effect" to guide the eye and add emphasis
- Balance 3D realism with artistic expression, avoid cartoonish exaggeration
- Use opacity or tints to emphasize one or two elements

### Aspirational Illustrations
- Emotive, intricate visuals for keynotes, homepages, hero campaigns
- Apply natural light, texture, and reflection
- Avoid exaggerating proportions or perspective

## Photography Guidelines
- No stock imagery
- Use only for specific people: customer stories, announcements, interviews
- Remove color and increase contrast to emphasize subject presence
- Key person from background for enhanced layout cohesion

## Logo Usage — Don'ts
- Don't misalign the mark and wordmark
- Don't use the default logo on a color background
- Don't fail contrast accessibility
- Don't alter, crop, skew, outline, or distort
- Don't exclude the mark from the logo
- Don't stack the logo vertically
- Don't add gradients or effects
- Don't substitute the logo in a sentence

## Color in Assets — Don'ts
- Don't use multiple colors in a single illustration
- Don't use colors for text
- Don't use multiple colors in a contiguous line
- Don't create gradients
- Don't pair floods of color

## Motion Principles
- **Build**: Layer and combine elements to imply growth, transformation, emergence
- **Enhance**: Zoom, crop, and focus on main ideas while guiding viewers through details
- **Reduce Friction**: Transition elements confidently at legible pace, seamless 3D/2D combinations
